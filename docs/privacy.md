## Part A — Privacy Policy

### 1. Data we collect

- **Account:** email, full name, 4-digit PIN (hashed), account numbers, IBAN/SWIFT
- **KYC (when provided):** identity documents, address proof, SOF declarations
- **Transactions:** amounts, currencies, counterparties, journal references
- **Technical:** IP address, device ID (sync), audit logs
- **Cards:** encrypted PAN/CVV vault (if issued)

### 2. How we use data

- Provide banking, swap, and chain services
- Comply with AML/CFT legal obligations
- Fraud prevention and transaction monitoring
- Customer support and dispute resolution

### 3. Legal bases (GDPR)

- **Contract** — service delivery
- **Legal obligation** — AML record-keeping
- **Legitimate interest** — security, fraud prevention

### 4. Sharing

We may share data with:

- **Hybx Finance / Fineract** (linked savings)
- **Payment partners** (Wise, Revolut, SWIFT correspondents, Marqeta)
- **KYC providers** (when integrated)
- **Custody providers** (Fireblocks, OneX, Alchemy)
- **Regulators** when legally required

We do **not** sell personal data.

### 5. Retention

See `docs/compliance/KYC-AML-FULL.md` §2.3 — typically 5–7 years post-relationship.

### 6. Your rights

Access, rectification, erasure (subject to AML holds), portability, objection — contact **privacy@anakatech.llc**.

### 7. International transfers

Data may be processed in the US, EU, and other regions where our infrastructure and partners operate. Appropriate safeguards (SCCs) apply where required.

### 1. Acceptance

By creating an account or using Nova Bank Online, you agree to these Terms and our KYC/AML program.

### 2. Eligibility

You must be 18+ (or local age of majority) and not prohibited from using financial services in your jurisdiction.

### 3. Services

Nova provides **ledger-backed accounts**, optional **real-rail payouts** (subject to KYC tier), **crypto vaults**, **card services**, and **ecosystem integrations**. Services may be sandbox or production depending on deployment configuration.

### 4. Account security

You are responsible for your email access and 4-digit PIN. Notify us immediately of unauthorized use.
